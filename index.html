Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material
properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion

Advanced extruders maintain uniform melt temperature for consistent wall thickness throughout the entire pipe length.

  • Temperature-controlled barrel
  • Continuous monitoring

Controlled Cooling

Gradual water cooling in calibration tanks prevents residual stress and ensures dimensional stability.

  • Calibrated water baths
  • Stress-free cooling

Vacuum Sizing

Precision vacuum tanks guarantee exact outer diameters within ±0.3% tolerance across all pressure class pipes.

  • Vacuum calibration
  • Diameter precision ±0.3%

Quality Control

Hydrostatic testing, dimensional checks, and material analysis at every production stage.

  • Hydrostatic pressure testing
  • Dimensional verification

Laser Marking

Permanent laser marking encodes specifications, certification marks and batch numbers on every pipe.

  • Laser-engraved markings
  • Full batch traceability

Precision Cutting

Automated cutting delivers clean, square-end cuts at specified lengths for optimal fusion welding performance.

  • Automated saw cutters
  • Square-end precision
